Early Ideas

Early on in the process we were inspired by plants, skin care, and Nina's clammy hands

Concept

We decided to use a house plant as our main subject, using sensors to detect sunlight and moisture levels of the soil

The first step was to get our sensors working. I got my soil sensor sensor working, and uploaded the data to Arduino IO. Uploaded it to my knob_feed because I was feeling lazy... but it worked :D

Xinxin also got her data uploaded to Arduino, and we sucessfully shared each other's data (easier said than done). We also got our servo motors working... each one controlled by a different sensor's data.

Arts and crafts time - building the body of our 'robot' out of paper mache

Video of our first working prototype - the right arm is controlled by the dampness of the soil sensor, and the left arm is controlled by a bend sensor

We ran into a couple of bugs that took us hours to figure out... Turns out a capital letter from the wifi password was missing... that was it. BUT we made a second version of our little robot, complete with 2 cute little arms.
